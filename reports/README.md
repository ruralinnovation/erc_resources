## Quick Reference: Q3 2025 Work Summary

### Two Primary Initiatives

**1. Network Mapping Tool Scoping**
- **Scope**: Analysis of 4 fellows, 21 partnerships from Q1 2025
- **Gap Analysis**: Four critical limitations identified (July 17)
- **Enhancement Roadmap**: Four Priority enhancements (+4-5 mins/fellow/quarter)
- **Technical Approach**: R, React, AWS; 520-570 hour buildout; embedded WordPress app
- **Status**: Design phase; IEDC finalizing approach before Thanksgiving

**2. Will Warren Capstone Project**
- **Focus**: Gap analysis across 8 Kentucky ADDs serving 54 Appalachian counties
- **Deliverables**: CEDS comparative analysis; platform architecture; impact measurement framework
- **CORI Support**: Data analysis, research methodology, platform guidance
- **Status**: Ongoing capstone support through ERC program

- **Lead Contact**: Darnesha Tabor (M&E Specialist)
